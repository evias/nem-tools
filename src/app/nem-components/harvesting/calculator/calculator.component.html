Introduction

Many people starting research about NEM are asking "what does vested balance mean?" and "how long does it take to get my XEM vested?".

  • NEM requires a vested balance of minimum 10000 XEM in order to be able to activate (delegated) harvesting
  • your unvested balance will automatically be vested at a rate of 10% each day
  • you will only be able to activate harvesting if your balance is > 10000 XEM
  • the less XEM are in your balance, the longer it will take to get your balance vested

With this tool you can easily calculate how much time it will take to get a specific amount of XEM vested

Note:current data may be provided by your address. you can also enter the values manually
