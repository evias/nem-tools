Automated restart of delegated harvesting

Regular headaches
  • delegated harvesting stops when a node is getting rebooted for some reason
  • you need to manually search for a node with free slots (can be frustrating using NanoWallet)
  • maybe you are on vacation and you cannot afford to restart harvesting
  • you miss the chance to harvest new blocks
Solution
  • we provide a service that automatically restarts delegated harvesting for you!
  • a scheduled task runs every 5 minutes and checks whether delegated harvesting for your account is still active
  • you will get notified via mail when delegated harvesting was restarted including the information about the host it is running on
What's needed?

    • detailed explanation how the service works and what you have to do
    • it's recommended to read this blog-entry before you start using this service
  • first of all you need to have delegated harvesting activated
    • tutorial (how to activate & start delegated harvesting in NanoWallet)
    • video-tutorial (also explains how to use this service)
  • when this is done we need 3 types of information from you
    • your e-mail address
    • public key of your delegated account
    • private key of you delegated account
      • Note: the funds in your regular account cannot be accessed by providing the private key of your delegated account
